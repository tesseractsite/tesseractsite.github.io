About Tesseract 4.0

TESSERACT is the Flagship Event of IEEE Student Branch of St.Josephs College Of Engineering And Technology Palai. This year TESSERACT 4.0 is back to illuminate every stage with your verve and vitality. It will be a fantastic platform for everyone to explore themselves by letting you to dive deeper into the mesmerising world of technologies as TESSERACT 4.0 comes with a plethora of Technical Workshops and Expert Talks. But it's not just that, the event also comes preloaded with lots of competitions and cultural programs to keep you amused and to instill in you a sense of happiness and joy throughout. The event is sponsored by IEEE Power and Energy society (PES), the world's largest platform for sharing the latest in technological developments in the electric power and Energy sector.

Workshops

Blockchain Workshop

In the world where we are dealing with internet fraud, malware and hacks, how can we trust the quality and intergrity of information that we get. The answer is Blockchain! A continuously growing chain of digital 'Blocks' of data. Blockchains are difficult to tamper with as they are decentralized and istributed across peer-to-peer networks that are continually updated and kept in sync. Join us for an interactive workshop for deep dive into this technology.

Electric Vehicle Workshop

Most automobile companies are vowing to switch to electric, a cleaner and sustainable alternative. An ‘electric’ revolution is coming to the automobile sector. There is a surge in demand for skilled workforce in electric and hybrid vehicle technologies. But, the industry is facing a shortage of aptly skilled workforce needed for the transition. This workshop will provide you advanced knowledge in design, analysis, control, calibration, and operating characteristics of EVs.

PLC Scada Workshop

Industrial Automation is vast developing system in the Current world. All type of industries depends PLC and and SCADA for controlling and monitoring the entire system. So now over period of time IOT, IIOT and AI type of technologies will merging with Operational Technologies like PLC SCADA and that is the reason future will be bright in Automation. There will be many job opportunities in IT as well as OT sectors as analyst, tester and developer for SCADA.

Leadership Exploration And Development Workshop

This workshop will help participants to identify the importance of effective management skills, as well as common obstacles to good time management. Participants will develop a deeper understanding of some of the common factors contributing to team work, as well as identify specific ways to manage their time more effectively. Also this workshop will help participants learn coping skills for managing stress, negative moods, and relationships.
